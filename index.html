Tic-Tac-Toe-ception

Rules of the game

It's a tic-tac-toe game, except each case is a tic-tac-toe game.

Game grid

The game grid is composed of a 9×9 grid, composed of 9 3×3 sub-grids, each containing a game of the classic tic-tac-toe.

Game Play

In order to win the game, a player must win three tic-tac-toe grids in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.

The player playing ❌ team starts the game by placing their mark on a grid cell. Then the player playing ⭕︎ team takes the turn.

When a player places their mark, it dictates in which sub-grid the next move must take place.

When a cell is marked, it is forbidden to mark it again, unless the sub-grid is already full and the player must place a move.

End of the game

The game stops either:

  • When a player wins. The player who has won three tic-tac-toe games in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.
  • When all the cases have been marked by either of the players. It is a draw.
